<p><p>The coin cell battery market consists of various types, each suited for specific applications. Lithium button cell batteries (CR) offer high energy density and are commonly used in watches and electronic devices. Silver oxide batteries (SR) provide stable voltage and are often preferred for precision instruments. Alkaline button cell batteries (LR) are cost-effective and widely used in basic electronics. Zinc-air batteries are popular in hearing aids due to their lightweight and high energy output. Other types include specialty batteries for niche applications.</p></p>
