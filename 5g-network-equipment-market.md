<p><p>The 5G network equipment market consists of various components essential for establishing robust connectivity. Macro cells provide wide-range coverage, while small cells enhance capacity in dense areas. Remote Radio Units (RRUs) and Active Antenna Units (AAUs) work together to transmit and receive signals efficiently. RF filters ensure signal clarity, and Baseband Units (BBUs) manage data processing. Phase shifters are pivotal for beamforming in advanced systems, and energy supply equipment supports the power needs of these high-performance technologies, ensuring seamless operation.</p></p>
