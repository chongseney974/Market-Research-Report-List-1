<p><p>The Lens Driver IC market is segmented into single channel, dual channel, and four channel types, based on the number of channels the integrated circuit can control. Single channel ICs drive one lens, ideal for basic applications. Dual channel ICs manage two lenses simultaneously, enhancing performance in systems requiring multiple focal points. Four channel ICs offer even greater flexibility, controlling four lenses, which is crucial for advanced imaging applications like multi-camera setups and sophisticated optical systems, enabling improved functionality and resolution.</p></p>
