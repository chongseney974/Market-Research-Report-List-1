<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Rechargeable Power Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nickle Cadmium (NiCd)</li><li>Nickle Metal Hydride (NiMH)</li><li>Lithium Ion (Li-Ion)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rechargeable power tools market includes several battery types. Nickel Cadmium (NiCd) batteries are known for their durability and ability to perform in extreme temperatures, but they suffer from memory effect and environmental concerns. Nickel Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries offer higher capacity and are more environmentally friendly than NiCd, but they can be bulkier. Lithium-Ion (Li-Ion) batteries dominate the market due to their lightweight, high energy density, and minimal self-discharge. Other market types may include newer technologies like lithium polymer or solid-state batteries.</p></p>
