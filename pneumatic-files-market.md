<p><ul><li>Reciprocating Engine</li><li>Vane Engine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic files market encompasses tools powered by compressed air, primarily categorized into reciprocating engines and vane engines. Reciprocating engines utilize back-and-forth motion to drive the file, offering precise control for detailed work, often in small-scale applications. Vane engines, on the other hand, rely on rotating vanes to deliver consistent power and speed, making them suitable for larger tasks requiring sustained performance. Both types enhance efficiency and precision in various industrial and manufacturing processes, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
