Technological advancements, such as the integration of GPS and telematics with speed limiters, are further enhancing their functionality and adoption.</p><p>Key trends in the market include the development of intelligent speed assistance (ISA) systems, which use real-time data to adjust vehicle speed based on road conditions and traffic.
