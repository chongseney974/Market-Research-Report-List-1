<p><p>The Vehicle Detectors market includes various types such as Inductive Loop, Ultrasonic, Magnetic, Radar, and Others. Inductive Loop detectors use electromagnetic fields to detect vehicles by sensing changes in inductance. Ultrasonic detectors rely on sound waves to measure vehicle presence. Magnetic detectors identify vehicles by detecting disturbances in the Earth's magnetic field. Radar detectors use radio waves to sense vehicle movement and speed. Other technologies include infrared, video-based, and piezoelectric systems, offering diverse solutions for traffic management and vehicle detection applications.</p></p>
