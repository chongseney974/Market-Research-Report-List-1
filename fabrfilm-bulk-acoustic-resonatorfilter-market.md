<p><ul><li>Membrane Type</li><li>Airgap Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FABR (Film Bulk Acoustic Resonator) filter market comprises two main types: membrane type and airgap type. Membrane type FABR filters utilize a thin membrane as the resonant structure, providing compactness and efficient performance for high-frequency applications. In contrast, airgap type FABR filters use an air gap to enhance acoustic resonance, resulting in improved selectivity and lower insertion loss. Both types are integral to advancements in wireless communications, IoT devices, and other technologies requiring precise frequency filtering.</p></p>
