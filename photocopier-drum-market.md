<p><ul><li>Organic Type</li><li>Inorganic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photocopier drum market is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic. Organic drums are typically made from polymer materials, offering better image quality and flexibility, making them suitable for high-volume printing. In contrast, inorganic drums are composed of metal oxides, providing durability and longevity, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Both types cater to different user needs, with organic drums excelling in vibrant color reproduction and inorganic drums focusing on cost-effectiveness and extended lifespan for intensive use.</p></p>
