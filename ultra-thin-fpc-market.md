<p><p>The ultra-thin flexible printed circuit (FPC) market is categorized into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided FPCs feature components and conductive pathways on one side, making them ideal for simpler applications with lower complexity. In contrast, double-sided FPCs have circuits on both sides, allowing for more intricate designs and higher component density. This dual-sided configuration enhances functionality and is commonly used in advanced electronics, including smartphones and wearables, where space optimization and performance are crucial.</p></p>
