<p><p>Aluminum foil for the industrial market is categorized into three types based on thickness: heavy gauge, medium gauge, and light gauge. Heavy gauge foil is thick and durable, ideal for applications requiring strength and barrier properties, such as food packaging and insulation. Medium gauge foil offers a balance between flexibility and strength, suited for flexible packaging and protective barriers. Light gauge foil is thinner and more cost-effective, commonly used in wrapping and general-purpose applications, where less durability is acceptable.</p></p>
