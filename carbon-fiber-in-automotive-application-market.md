<p><p>The automotive application market for carbon fiber includes various manufacturing processes. Resin Transfer Molding (RTM) involves injecting resin into a fiber preform, offering high strength and complexity. Vacuum Infusion Processing uses a vacuum to draw resin into dry fibers, ensuring uniform distribution. Injection Molding allows for rapid production of complex shapes using a polymer matrix. Compression Molding forms carbon fiber parts under heat and pressure, ideal for producing large components. Other methods encompass emerging technologies and hybrid approaches for specialized applications.</p></p>
