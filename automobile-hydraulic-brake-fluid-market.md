<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automobile Hydraulic Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT 3 Brake Fluids</li><li>DOT 4 Brake Fluids</li><li>DOT 5 Brake Fluids</li><li>DOT 5.1 Brake Fluids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile hydraulic brake fluid market comprises several types: DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1. DOT 3 is glycol-based, suitable for most passenger vehicles but has a lower boiling point. DOT 4 also uses glycol but offers better performance under higher temperatures and is common in both cars and light trucks. DOT 5 is silicone-based, not hygroscopic, ideal for classic vehicles but incompatible with other fluids. DOT 5.1 is glycol-based with a higher boiling point, suitable for high-performance applications.</p></p>
