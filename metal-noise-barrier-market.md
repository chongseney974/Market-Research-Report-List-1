<p><p>The Metal Noise Barrier market is segmented into two main types: Ground-Mounted Noise Barriers and Structure-Mounted Noise Barriers. Ground-Mounted Noise Barriers are installed directly on the ground, providing a physical barrier that reduces sound transmission from roadways or industrial areas. Meanwhile, Structure-Mounted Noise Barriers are attached to existing structures like buildings or bridges, enhancing sound insulation without requiring extensive ground modification. Both types effectively mitigate noise pollution, contributing to improved urban environments and ensuring compliance with noise regulations.</p></p>
