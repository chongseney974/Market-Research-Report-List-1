<p><p>Inverting regulators, which convert a positive input voltage to a negative output voltage, primarily fall into two market types: integrated switch and external switch. Integrated switch regulators feature an embedded switch within the chip, offering compact solutions ideal for space-constrained applications. External switch regulators, on the other hand, utilize separate switching components, enabling enhanced flexibility and performance for high-power applications. These markets cater to diverse needs in consumer electronics, industrial systems, and telecommunications, driven by varying power requirements and design preferences.</p></p>
