<p><p>The Flame Retardant ABS Resin market is categorized into two main types: non-halogen type and halogen type. Non-halogen type resins, made without halogenated compounds, offer fewer toxic emissions and are increasingly favored for environmental compliance and safety. In contrast, halogen type resins contain bromine or chlorine compounds, providing effective flame resistance but raising concerns about toxicity in case of fire. Each type serves distinct applications in electronics, automotive, and construction, driven by regulatory requirements and safety standards.</p></p>
