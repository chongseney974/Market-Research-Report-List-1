<p><p>Polyvinylidene Chloride (PVDC) is a versatile polymer primarily used in packaging due to its excellent barrier properties. The market for PVDC is divided into two types: isotactic and atactic. Isotactic PVDC exhibits a high level of crystallinity, enhancing its strength and thermal stability, making it suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, atactic PVDC has a more amorphous structure, providing flexibility and clarity, ideal for applications requiring transparency. Both types serve specific needs in industries like food packaging and construction.</p></p>
