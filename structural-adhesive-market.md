<p><p>The structural adhesive market includes various types, each with distinct properties. **Epoxy** adhesives offer high strength, durability, and resistance to chemicals, ideal for heavy-duty applications. **Polyurethane** adhesives provide flexibility, impact resistance, and bonding for diverse substrates, including plastics and metals. **Acrylic** adhesives combine fast curing, strong bonds, and resistance to environmental factors. **Methyl Methacrylate (MMA)** adhesives excel in bonding dissimilar materials with high strength and durability. **Cyanoacrylate** adhesives, known as "super glues," offer rapid bonding for small-scale, precise applications. Each type caters to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
