<p><strong>The Rutile Titanium Dioxide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Chloride Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rutile Titanium Dioxide market is primarily divided into two production processes: the sulfate and chloride processes. The sulfate process involves treating titanium ore with sulfuric acid, leading to the production of titanium dioxide with varying particle sizes and properties. In contrast, the chloride process uses chlorine gas to produce titanium tetrachloride, which is subsequently oxidized to yield high-purity titanium dioxide. Each method has distinct advantages and applications, influencing market dynamics and product characteristics.</p></p>
