<p><p>The Flame Retardant Polyamide Fibre market features two primary types: Copolymerization and Blending Method. Copolymerization involves the chemical combination of two or more monomers to create polyamide fibers with inherent flame retardant properties, enhancing safety and performance. The Blending Method, on the other hand, involves mixing traditional polyamide fibers with flame retardant additives to achieve the desired fire-resistant characteristics. Both methods aim to produce fibers suitable for applications in textiles, automotive, and construction, ensuring compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
