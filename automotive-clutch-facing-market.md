<p><p>The automotive clutch facing market encompasses two main types: dry type and wet type clutch facings. Dry type clutch facings operate without lubrication, relying on friction for engagement and are commonly found in manual transmissions due to their simplicity and efficiency. In contrast, wet type clutch facings are immersed in oil, providing better cooling and reduced wear, making them ideal for high-performance applications and automatic transmissions. Each type serves distinct automotive needs, influencing performance and durability.</p></p>
