<p><strong>The CRT Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Random-Scan displays</li><li>Raster-scan displays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CRT monitor market is primarily categorized into two types: random-scan displays and raster-scan displays. Random-scan displays, also known as vector displays, render images by drawing lines directly on the screen, making them ideal for graphics that require high precision, such as CAD applications. Conversely, raster-scan displays work by illuminating pixels in a grid format, which is suited for displaying complex images and videos, commonly used in televisions and general-purpose monitors. Each type serves distinct applications and user preferences.</p></p>
