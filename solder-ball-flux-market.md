<p><p>The solder ball flux market comprises three main types: Rosin Flux, Water Soluble Flux, and No-Clean Flux. Rosin Flux, derived from natural resins, provides excellent wetting and is suitable for traditional applications. Water Soluble Flux utilizes chemical formulations that can be easily cleaned with water, making it ideal for high-reliability electronics. No-Clean Flux, designed to leave minimal residue, eliminates the need for cleaning and is perfect for high-speed production where post-soldering cleaning is impractical. Each type caters to specific application needs in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
