<p><p>Magnesium oxide single crystal substrates are categorized into two market types based on their polishing methods: single side polishing and both side polishing. Single side polished substrates feature a finely finished surface on one side, making them suitable for applications where only one surface requires precision. In contrast, both side polished substrates are uniformly finished on both sides, enhancing their versatility for complex applications in electronics and optics, where dual-surface quality is essential for performance and accuracy.</p></p>
