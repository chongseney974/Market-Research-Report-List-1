<p><p>Bacterial diagnostics in aquaculture encompass various market types to detect and manage bacterial infections in aquatic species. Molecular diagnostics utilize nucleic acid-based techniques, such as polymerase chain reaction (PCR), to identify specific pathogens rapidly. Immunofluorescent antibody tests (IFAT) employ fluorescence-labeled antibodies to visualize bacteria in samples. Other methods may include culture techniques, biochemical tests, or serology. These diagnostic tools ensure early detection, effective treatment, and improved biosecurity in aquaculture, ultimately enhancing fish health and production efficiency.</p></p>
