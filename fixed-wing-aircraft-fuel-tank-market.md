<p><ul><li>Internal</li><li>External</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fixed Wing Aircraft Fuel Tank Market includes internal and external fuel tanks. Internal tanks are integrated within the aircraft's structure, offering aerodynamic efficiency and reduced drag. They are commonly used in commercial and military aircraft for long-range operations. External tanks, also known as drop tanks, are mounted outside the aircraft and can be jettisoned when empty or during combat. They provide additional fuel capacity for extended missions but may increase drag and reduce maneuverability. Both types cater to specific operational needs.</p></p>
