<p><p>The fluorinated resins market includes several key types such as PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), known for its excellent chemical resistance and non-stick properties; PCTFE (Polychlorotrifluoroethylene), valued for its low permeability and thermal stability; PVDF (Polyvinylidene fluoride), recognized for its toughness and chemical resistance; ETFE (Ethylene tetrafluoroethylene), which offers high flexibility and strength; and ECTFE (Ethylene chlorotrifluoroethylene), combining toughness with chemical resistance. Other fluorinated resins also exist, catering to niche applications across various industries, including chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and electronics.</p></p>
