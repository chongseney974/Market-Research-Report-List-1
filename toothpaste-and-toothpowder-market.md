<p><p>The toothpaste and toothpowder market comprises several types based on their abrasive agents. Silica type products use silica as a gentle abrasive, providing a milder cleaning effect. Calcium carbonate type is effective in stain removal and offers a rougher texture. Phosphate type contains phosphates that help in strengthening enamel and reducing sensitivity. Aluminum hydroxide type serves as a softer abrasive, enhancing texture without harming gums. The "Other" category includes unique formulations with alternative ingredients for specific dental concerns.</p></p>
