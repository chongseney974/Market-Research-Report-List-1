<p><p>The flat bed screen printing machine market is classified into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require human intervention for operation, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity, but with lower throughput. Semi-automatic machines combine manual and automated processes, improving efficiency while still needing operator input. Fully automatic machines operate with minimal human involvement, providing high-speed production and consistency, making them ideal for large-scale manufacturing. Each type caters to different production needs and budget considerations in the printing industry.</p></p>
