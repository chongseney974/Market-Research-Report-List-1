<p><p>The gravure inks market consists of various types, including water-based, alcoholic, benzene-based, and petrol-based inks. Water type gravure inks are eco-friendly, offering low VOC emissions and easy cleanup. Alcoholic gravure inks feature quick drying times and high-quality prints suitable for flexible packaging. Benzene type gravure inks deliver vibrant colors but raise health concerns due to toxic solvents. Petrol type inks provide excellent adhesion and durability, often used for high-performance applications, but have similar health and environmental considerations as benzene inks.</p></p>
