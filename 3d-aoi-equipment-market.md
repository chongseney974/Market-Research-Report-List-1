<p><p>The 3D AOI equipment market comprises two primary types: offline and inline automated optical inspection systems. Offline AOI equipment operates independently from the production line, allowing for detailed inspections of assembled PCBs after manufacturing. In contrast, inline AOI equipment integrates seamlessly into the production process, enabling real-time inspection during assembly. This real-time capability helps identify defects early, enhancing overall quality control and efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring product reliability and minimizing defects in electronic manufacturing.</p></p>
