<p><p>Automotive gear oils are classified by viscosity grades, which indicate their flow characteristics at different temperatures. The "W" denotes winter, showing performance in cold conditions. **75W** and **80W** are thinner oils, ideal for colder climates, ensuring smooth gear operation. **85W** balances cold and warm weather performance. **90** is a common all-season grade, suitable for moderate temperatures. **140** is thicker, designed for high-temperature or heavy-load applications, providing enhanced protection under stress. Each grade ensures optimal lubrication and protection for gears, tailored to specific driving conditions and vehicle requirements.</p></p>
