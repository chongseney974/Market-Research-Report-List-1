<p><strong>The Frozen Fresh Meat Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Packaging</li><li>Modified Atmosphere Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Frozen Fresh Meat Packaging Market includes two primary types: Vacuum Packaging and Modified Atmosphere Packaging. Vacuum Packaging involves sealing meat in airtight bags, removing air to extend shelf life and prevent freezer burn. In contrast, Modified Atmosphere Packaging replaces the air inside the packaging with a mixture of gases like carbon dioxide and nitrogen, which helps slow down spoilage and maintain quality. Both methods are essential for preserving the freshness and safety of meat during storage and transportation.</p></p>
