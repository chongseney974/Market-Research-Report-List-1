<p><strong>The Plastic Corrugated Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Wall Corrugated</li><li>Double Wall Corrugated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic corrugated pipe market is primarily divided into two types: single wall corrugated and double wall corrugated pipes. Single wall corrugated pipes are lightweight and flexible, making them ideal for applications like drainage and landscaping. In contrast, double wall corrugated pipes feature an inner smooth wall that enhances fluid flow and an outer corrugated layer for added strength, making them suitable for larger-scale drainage systems and applications requiring structural integrity. Both types are valued for their durability and resistance to environmental factors.</p></p>
