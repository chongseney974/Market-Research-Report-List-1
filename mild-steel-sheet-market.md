<p><strong>The Mild Steel Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Roll Mild Steel Sheet</li><li>Cold Roll Mild Steel Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Hot Roll Mild Steel Sheet** market involves steel sheets produced by rolling heated steel slabs, resulting in a rough surface and less precise dimensions. These sheets are cost-effective and commonly used in construction, automotive frames, and machinery where surface finish isn't critical. The **Cold Roll Mild Steel Sheet** market focuses on sheets processed at room temperature, offering smoother surfaces, tighter tolerances, and better strength. They are ideal for appliances, automotive panels, and precision engineering, where aesthetics and dimensional accuracy are essential. Both markets cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
