<p><p>Directional valves control fluid flow in hydraulic systems and are classified into several types. Motorized valves use electric motors for operation, offering precise control and automation. Electromagnetic valves utilize solenoids to actuate movement, providing quick response times. Electro-hydraulic valves combine electric and hydraulic mechanisms for enhanced efficiency and control in complex systems. Manual valves require physical intervention for operation, allowing for straightforward control without electrical components, making them simple yet effective for various applications.</p></p>
