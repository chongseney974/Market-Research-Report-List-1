<p><p>The PU microfiber leather market is primarily segmented into two types: solvent-based and water-based PU microfiber leather. Solvent PU microfiber leather utilizes organic solvents in its production, resulting in a durable and versatile product, often preferred for high-performance applications. In contrast, water-based PU microfiber leather is crafted using water as a base, making it more environmentally friendly and low in volatile organic compounds. Both types cater to diverse industries, including fashion, automotive, and upholstery, balancing durability and sustainability.</p></p>
