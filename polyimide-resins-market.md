<p><strong>The Polyimide Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastics</li><li>Thermosetting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyimide resins market is divided into two primary types: thermoplastics and thermosetting. Thermoplastic polyimides are characterized by their ability to be remelted and reshaped, offering flexibility and processing advantages. They are commonly used in applications requiring high-temperature resistance and durability. In contrast, thermosetting polyimides undergo a chemical change during curing, resulting in a strong, stable structure that cannot be reprocessed. These resins excel in high-performance applications, such as aerospace and electronics, due to their excellent mechanical and thermal properties.</p></p>
