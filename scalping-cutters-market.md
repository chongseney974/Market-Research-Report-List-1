<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Spiral</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Scalping cutters are specialized tooling used in machining processes for producing flat surfaces, typically featuring two primary market types: straight and spiral. Straight scalping cutters have parallel teeth aligned directly along the axis, offering efficient cutting for specific applications. In contrast, spiral scalping cutters possess teeth arranged in a helical shape, facilitating smoother cutting action and reducing vibrations. Both types are essential for enhancing productivity and accuracy in manufacturing, catering to different material removal requirements and operational preferences.</p></p>
